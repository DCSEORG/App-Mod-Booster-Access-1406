How we use your data

We will use your data for the purposes of providing your quote which may include:

  • Sharing your data with our insurers.
  • Using your data for fraud prevention and to verify the information you provide to us.
  • Checks with external databases such as credit reference agencies and publicly available sources of information as part of this process. Any credit searches will appear on your credit report but will not be visible to other lenders, unless you take out a policy and choose to pay by monthly instalments.

If you have previously held a product with BISL Limited or obtained a quote in the past, we may carry out checks against the data we already hold on you.
